Speakers

Thien Nguyen

Thien is Engineering Manager at NashTech, a Microsoft MVP and the creator of SimplCommerce. He is a passionate developer, a mentor and a speaker. His current interests are .NET Core, Azure and Microservices

Thang Chung

Thang has over 10-years’ experience in the software development industry. He works as a senior cloud solution architect on Azure Cloud Platform at NashTech company. He is a creator and maintainer of many projects on his GitHub account such as awesome-dotnetcore, clean-code-dotnet, coolstore-microservices, cloud-native-kit...

Phuong Le

Phuong is an experienced, hands-on software development enthusiast who has been involved in the architecture, design and implementation of multiple business systems across a variety of domains in the last 15 years. He is currently an Engineering Manager at NashTech Global, a UK-based leading software outsourcing company specialised in enterprise-graded business application systems. Phuong’s passion in applied technologies is as great as in people development; which has been materialised in his recent success in the development of both a highly complex microservices system and a technical-capable development team. He is a regular technical trainer in the organisation and occasion conference speaker in the Vietnam Software Community. At the moment, Phuong is passionate in creating cloud-first, distributed enterprise solutions out of maximising the potential of DevOps and Kubernetes.

Thanh Tran

Working at NashTech as Principal Software Engineer, has over 7 years of experience in software development, interested in building a scalable, resilient and high performance application. Mainly focus on microservices and serverless architecture build on top of PaaS like MS Azure and Google Cloud Platform.
